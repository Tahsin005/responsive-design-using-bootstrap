About me

What do you want to know?

I'm MD. Tahsin Ferdous, a passionate software developer with a focus on backend development and a background in competitive programming. My journey in programming began with the thrill of competitive coding challenges, where I honed my problem-solving skills and developed a deep understanding of algorithms and data structures.

More about me

I have a diverse range of interests that extend beyond software development. When I'm not coding, you can often find me pursuing my passions in speed cubing, football analytics, and competitive programming.

Speed cubing is not just a hobby for me; it's a mental challenge that I find incredibly rewarding. Solving Rubik's cubes and other twisty puzzles has taught me patience, perseverance, and problem-solving skills that I apply to my coding projects.

As a football enthusiast, I enjoy diving deep into the world of sports analytics. Analyzing player performance, game strategies, and match statistics fascinates me, and I'm always on the lookout for innovative approaches to football analytics.

Competitive programming has been a cornerstone of my journey as a software developer. Participating in coding competitions sharpens my algorithmic thinking and keeps me motivated to continuously improve my coding skills.

These interests not only provide me with a well-rounded perspective but also fuel my curiosity and drive for excellence in everything I do.

I have a strong academic background in the field of computer science. I completed my SSC from Cox's Bazar Govt. High School and my HSC from Cox's Bazar Govt. College, where I developed a solid foundation in mathematics, science, and technology.

Currently, I am pursuing a B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering at City University. This program has provided me with a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, software development, and problem-solving techniques. I am passionate about learning and applying cutting-edge technologies to solve real-world problems.

My educational journey has equipped me with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the field of computer science, and I am excited to continue my academic and professional development in this dynamic and ever-evolving field.

My journey in the world of programming is guided by ambitious goals and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Here are some of my aspirations:

  • Become a Specialist on Codeforces: My dream is to excel in competitive programming and achieve the prestigious title of Specialist on Codeforces. I aim to showcase my problem-solving skills and algorithmic expertise on this renowned platform.
  • Reach 3-Star Coder on CodeChef: I aspire to demonstrate my proficiency in competitive programming by attaining the title of a 3-star coder on CodeChef. This milestone reflects my commitment to solving complex problems efficiently and effectively.
  • Solve 1000 Problems on Codeforces: To continually challenge myself and expand my problem-solving abilities, I have set a goal to solve 1000 problems on Codeforces. Each solved problem contributes to my growth as a programmer and prepares me for future coding challenges.
  • Participate in the ICPC Dhaka Regional - 2024: I am eager to collaborate with talented individuals and compete at the highest level of collegiate programming by participating in the ICPC Dhaka Regional - 2024. This competition provides an opportunity to test my skills and learn from fellow participants.
  • Become a Backend Engineer: In addition to my passion for competitive programming, I am dedicated to pursuing a career as a backend engineer. I am drawn to the challenges of building scalable, efficient, and robust backend systems that power modern web applications.

With determination and perseverance, I am committed to achieving these goals and making a meaningful impact in the field of computer science.
